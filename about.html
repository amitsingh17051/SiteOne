Our Company

Clothing is the basic necessity for humans. Introduction of new fashion is attracting consumers to go for readymade garments which offer designs in line with the latest trends. A continuous improvement in literacy ratio and shift to urban areas also leads to change in clothing preferences with more population opting for readymade garments.

Readymade garments are the choice of urban people but It is also gaining wider acceptance in semi-urban and rural areas. The huge charges made by tailors and delay in delivery have made people to switch over to readymade garments. In domestic market and export market, it has made spectacular progress in the last decade.
